## üéØ PROJECT VISION

**Three Problems. One Platform. Permanently On-Chain.**

### Problem 1: Lost Cultural Origins
DJ Kool Herc was credited as "creator of hip-hop" until Tariq Nasheed's "Microphone Check" documented the actual originators. **Solution:** Immutable timestamps on Arweave prevent revisionist history.

### Problem 2: Missed IP Rights
Birdman coined "Bling Bling" but missed the trademark window. **Solution:** Permanent proof of "first use" enables creators to own their innovations.

### Problem 3: "I Did It First" Claims
Soulja Boy says he invented everything. **Solution:** Kulture Wire traces the actual first tweet/video/claim with forensic proof.
